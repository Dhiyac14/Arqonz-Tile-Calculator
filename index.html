How To Use Tile Calculator

  1. STEP 1: Let us know whether you are looking for floor or wall tiles.
  2. STEP 2: Let us know the total area of the space. Please ensure to deduct the area of any doors or windows to arrive at the final area. Please enter the area in either Sq. Feet or Sq. Metre only.
  3. STEP 3: Select the size of the tile you want from the dropdown option and click Calculate.
  4. STEP 4: You will get the total number of tiles required or total number of boxes of tiles required.

Note: Total tiles required are adjusted by 10% considering for wastage and design matching. It is assumed 4 tiles per box as a standard to calculate boxes.
